How to use:

  1. Enter a room ID and click "Join Room"
  2. Share the room ID with the person you want to call
  3. The first person to join becomes the caller
  4. Click "Start Call" to initiate the video call
  5. The second person will receive the call and can click "Answer"
  6. Use the camera and microphone buttons to toggle during the call
